Men's Shoes

Boat

Boat

Typically canvas or leather shoes with non-marking rubber soles. A sipping pattern is cut into the soles to provide grip on a wet deck.

Chelsea

Chelsea

These are close-fitting, ankle-high boots with an elastic side panel. It often have a loop or tab of fabric on back of boot.

Chukkas

Chukkas

These are ankle-high shoes with suede or leather uppers, leather or rubber soles and open lacing with two or three pairs of eyelets.

Derby

Derby

Shoes with shoelace eyelets sewn on top of the vamp. There is no seam between the tongue and front of the shoe.

Loafer

Loafer

It's a slip-on shoe having no laces. Ankle is exposed and the shoe doesn't wrap snugly.

Monkstrap

Monkstrap

Shoes with no lacing. It is secured on the feet by one or multiple buckles and straps.

Oxfords

Oxford

Plain and formal leather shoes with shoelace eyelets attached under the vamp.

Sneakers

Sneakers

Primarily designed for sports. Also known as trainers, kicks, runners, athletic shoes etc. Quite popular for casuals.

Wingtip

Wingtip

Any shoes with toecup extended with low wings that reach around the side of the shoe.
