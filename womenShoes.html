Women's Shoes

Stilettos

Stilettos

Long, thin, high heel found on boots and shoes. Named after stilettos dagger it's length varies from 1-10 inches and diameter is usually less than 0.5 inch.

Ballet Flats

Ballet Flats

Lightweight shoes inspired by ballet shoes with very little or no heel.

Platform

Platform

With an obvious thick sole usually in 3-10 cm range. Soles can have a uniform thickness, a wedge or a stilettos.

Jelly Shoes

Jelly Shoes

These are made up of pure PVC, plastic frequently infused with glitters. It's name refers to semi-transparent material with jelly-like sheen.

Gladiator

Gladiator

Flat sandals with several wide cross-straps holding the sole to the foot.

Loafer

Loafer

It's a slip-on shoe having no laces. Ankle is exposed and the shoe doesn't wrap snugly.

Mules

Mules

Backless, closed-toed shoes with no constraints around foot's heel.

Wedges

Wedges

Shoes with sole in the form of a wedge such that one type of material usually rubber serves both the sole and the heel.

Pumps

Pumps

Court shoe with low-cut front or vamp with either a shoe buckle or a bow as ostensible fastening.
